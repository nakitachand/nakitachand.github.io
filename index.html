Augmented Reality Watch Try-on

Try-on watches in different colors in AR!

Albeit a simple app, this project tied in many elements that are essential to building great AR apps. Among them included image tracking, occlusion, material rendering, UI design, and animation. Also, each surface of the 3D watch models were rendered in Unity to achieve the desired look, including the use of light probes on the dial glass for a realistic reflection.
